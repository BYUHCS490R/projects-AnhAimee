Utah

Utah is known for its stunning national parks, red rock formations, mountains, and outdoor adventures. From hiking in Zion to skiing in Salt Lake City, Utah offers some of the most beautiful natural landscapes in the United States.

Activities like hiking, skiing, and camping attract thousands of visitors each year.
