Philippine

The Philippines is an archipelago in Southeast Asia made up of over 7,600 islands. It is known for its stunning beaches, tropical climate, vibrant culture, and warm, friendly people. With a mix of natural beauty, historical sites, and exciting outdoor activities, the Philippines is a dream destination for travelers.
