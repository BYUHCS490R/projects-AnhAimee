Hawaii

Hawaii is one of the most unique places in the world, known for its breathtaking natural landscapes, vibrant culture, and welcoming spirit. Located in the middle of the Pacific Ocean, it is the only U.S. state made entirely of islands and the only one with a tropical climate. Hawaii is made up of eight major islands, with Oahu, Maui, Big Island (Hawaii Island), and Kauai being the most visited.
