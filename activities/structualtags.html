Structual Tags

  • In the headercontains logo, site name, navigation bars, search bar or call-to-action buttons
  • In the Nav is a section that helps the browsers move between different sections. It contains drop down menus or links to main pages such as home, about, services, contact, or shop. The Nav can goes above, below, or within the header.
  • In the Mainis a central part of a website, where contains the main content such as text content, images, videos, or anyform of content. The main section changes from page to page
  • In the Footeris uausally located at the bottom section of a webpage and it appears on every page. It contains supporting information such as contact information, copyright notice, social media links, or logo.
