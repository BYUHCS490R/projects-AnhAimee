FAMILY MEMBERS

Fazhou

Fazhou is Mulan's father. he is a wise, gentle, and honorable man. He is a respected war veteran who had injury from his old accicent but despite that, he still contributes to his country.

Fali

Fali is Mulan's mother. She is a caring and traditional woman. She is always there to support her husband and her family. However, she worries more about Mulan's rebellious nature. She personally wants Mulan to find happiness.

Mulan

Mulan is the main character in the story. She is a brave, intelligent, and independent woman. Mulan disguises herself to be a man in her father's army when the war breaks out. She wants to prove her courage to all people.
